# Laptop-Market-Insights
Power BI dashboard analyzing Flipkart laptop data to uncover insights on pricing, ratings, brand performance, and customer preferences.

Flipkart Laptop Sales Dashboard
Project Overview

This project analyzes laptop listings on Flipkart to understand pricing trends, brand performance, and customer preferences. The dashboard provides insights into product distribution, ratings, and discount impact.

Objectives

Analyze laptop pricing and ratings
Identify top-performing brands and processors
Understand the impact of discounts on customer engagement
Detect best value products

Key Insights

Average laptop price is ₹53.6K with an average rating of 4.19
HP, ASUS, and Dell dominate the listings
Most laptops fall in the mid-price range
Intel processors are the most commonly used
Moderate discounts (20–40%) lead to better ratings and engagement
Mid-range laptops with high ratings offer the best value
